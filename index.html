WHO I AM?

I am James Bernard Oplado Gerena, hailing from the beautiful province of Eastern Samar. Family and friends are the core of my life, and their presence fills my heart with joy and happiness. I cherish the moments spent with them, creating unforgettable memories and sharing laughter and love.

Nature beckons me with its alluring charm, and I find solace and adventure in hiking and exploring its breathtaking landscapes. The serenity of the mountains, the soothing sound of flowing rivers, and the whisper of the wind captivate my soul, reminding me of the boundless beauty that surrounds us.

Dancing is my passion, an art form that allows me to express myself freely. Whether it's graceful ballet or energetic hip-hop, the rhythm flows through my veins, igniting my spirit and letting me connect with my emotions in a unique and captivating way.

Playing brings out the child within me, be it engaging in sports or indulging in fun-filled activities. The thrill of competition and the camaraderie shared with teammates fills my days with excitement and a sense of fulfillment.

Manga or manhwa, anime, and movies transport me to fantastical worlds brimming with imagination and wonder. They ignite my imagination, inspire me, and provide a form of escapism from the complexities of everyday life. The artistry, storytelling, and captivating characters found in these mediums captivate my mind and heart, leaving an indelible impression.

In a nutshell, my life revolves around the love and warmth of my family and friends, the exploration of nature's wonders, the expression of dance, the joy of playing, and the immersive experiences offered by manga, manhwa, anime, and movies. Each of these elements enriches my life, shaping me into the person I am today.

ELEMENTARY

During my elementary days, I had the incredible opportunity to experience and discover a multitude of new things. One of the things I loved most was playing with my friends, creating lasting memories that still bring a smile to my face today. Whether it was during recess or after school, we would engage in various games and activities, fostering bonds and learning the importance of teamwork.

One area where I truly excelled was in dancing. Elementary school provided me with the platform to enhance my talent and passion for dancing. I had the chance to participate in school events and competitions, showcasing my skills and building my confidence on stage. The thrill of performing and the applause of the audience ignited a fire within me, encouraging me to continue pursuing my passion for dance.

Apart from dancing, I also had the opportunity to join extracurricular activities such as the Boy's Scout. Engaging in scouting activities allowed me to develop essential life skills, including leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving. It was a valuable experience that shaped my character and provided a strong foundation for my personal growth.

Furthermore, I enjoyed engaging in board games and physical games during my elementary days. These activities not only provided entertainment but also helped improve my critical thinking, strategic planning, and physical fitness. Whether it was a game of chess, tag, or soccer, each interaction offered valuable lessons and fostered a sense of healthy competition.

My elementary days were a time of exploration, friendship, and personal growth. They enabled me to discover my talents, build strong connections with others, and cultivate important life skills. The memories and experiences I gained during this period have had a lasting impact on my journey of self-discovery and continue to shape the person I am today.

HIGHSCHOOL

❮ ❯

My high school days were a transformative period that allowed me to expand my horizons and experience a world beyond what I knew in my elementary years. One of the most enriching aspects was the opportunity to meet new people and gain a diverse circle of friends. Interacting with individuals from different backgrounds and perspectives broadened my understanding of the world and taught me the value of empathy and acceptance.

Hanging out with friends was a cherished part of my high school experience. Together, we created countless memories that I will forever hold dear. Whether it was engaging in spontaneous adventures, sharing laughter during lunch breaks, or supporting each other through the ups and downs of teenage life, these moments fostered deep bonds and formed the foundation of lifelong friendships.

One of my passions during high school was dancing, and I had the privilege to showcase my talent in various events. Whether it was performing in school assemblies or participating in dance competitions, the stage became my second home. Dancing allowed me to express myself creatively and connect with others through the power of movement. The applause and support from the audience filled me with a sense of fulfillment and motivated me to continue honing my skills.

High school also provided opportunities to participate in exciting events like intramurals and cheer dances. These events brought together students from different grade levels, fostering a sense of unity and healthy competition. Engaging in sports and cheerleading activities taught me the importance of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. The electrifying atmosphere and the cheers of fellow classmates created an unforgettable experience that epitomized the spirit of high school camaraderie.

Ultimately, my high school journey culminated in a significant milestone: my graduation. This unforgettable moment marked the culmination of years of hard work, growth, and personal development. Walking across the stage, receiving my diploma, and celebrating this achievement with my classmates and loved ones instilled in me a profound sense of pride and optimism for the future.

My high school days were a time of exploration, friendship, personal achievements, and unforgettable memories. From meeting new people and gaining lifelong friends to dancing at events and participating in school-wide celebrations, each experience enriched my life and contributed to my personal growth. Graduating high school became a testament to my dedication and resilience, providing a solid foundation for the exciting journey that lay ahead.
